Title: The Transient Pipeline (TraP) and AARTFAAC

Abstract:

This talk will  give an overview of the LOFAR  Transients Pipeline (TraP), which
we use to  automatically process AARTFAAC images  in close to real  time. I will
highlight  the  new features  developed  for  AARTFAAC  and key  data  products.
Finally,  I  will   describe  our  future  development   plans  including  speed
improvements, alerts and triggering capabilities.
